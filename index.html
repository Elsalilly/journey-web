About me

Hi! My name is Elsa and I love to travel. To be able to visit new places and immerse myself into new cultures is truly something that brings me joy. Right now I live in Malmö with my partner, and I am studying a course in front-end development.



Dreamy waters, beaches and snorkelling

Sunshine, swimming in crystal blue waters and delicious food. This could definitely describe the Philippines.
Our last stop was Malapascua, an remote island surrounded in this gorgeous environment. I tried diving
for the first time ever and it was both scary and so much fun. I will definitely do it again during our trip.
We have mostly been hanging out at the beach, and we actually stayed at a small resort for the first time as well.
They even had a pool so this was a luxurious stay for us. We also did some snorkelling around the island and saw some fishes and beautiful corals.




Food, food and more food, yes please

Food has been one of the contributing factors that we are so in love with Taiwan. All the night markets, dumplings, noodles and pastries.
Some of the things that we tried were boba tea, which was Lisa favourite especially with tapioca pearls. Deep fried sweet potato balls,
Udon noodles, ice cream with coriander and peanuts wrapped in a crêpe-variant, stinky tofu, sufflé pancakes and wontons.
However our all time favourite was xiao long bao, which is a soup dumpling.
But Taiwan also had amazing arts and culture centers we could visit. Also, a long history that we deep dived into.







5:am and just a normal morning?

Today, we have been in Thailand for an entire week and we have managed to do a lot of things. But we have to start at the beginning.
Last monday, we went up super early so we could catch the bus at 04.40. We went to Kastrup and jumped on our first plane.
Where we actually had good plane food, saw movies and of course slept a little. On the second plane, there's wasn't much sleep.
So the sleep deprivation was an absolute fact, but at 07.40 we landed in bangkok.
